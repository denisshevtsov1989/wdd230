The goal of the Chamber of Commerce is to attract young entrepreneurs and help develop an entrepreneurial culture and business development, which will expand the area of interaction between different business areas.

Alexander is a young entrepreneur in the field of development and implementation of scientific and technical developments. It is important to attract new ideas and investments for business development and attract new people to create new products that can soon be used by people in various fields.

Person №2: Max

Max has long been fond of art. Making new commercial connections helps Max not only develop his own business, but also allows other entrepreneurs and large businesses to create a special atmosphere by creating an interior using the art objects that Max provides.
