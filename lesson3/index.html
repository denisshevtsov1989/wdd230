Purpose

Priority tasks and activities of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry: active promotion of the development and wide application of innovations, modern technologies, knowledge economy; representation and protection of the interests of business circles in public authorities and local self-government; active participation in the development of legislative and other regulatory legal acts affecting the interests of entrepreneurs, and the creation of an effective system of independent expert evaluation of their projects; participation in the formation and development of the infrastructure for servicing entrepreneurship, expanding the scope of services provided by the chambers, improving their quality and high degree of accessibility for business, public and government structures, municipalities; establishment and development of relations with foreign partner organizations and business circles, as well as international business organizations; promotion of effective and high-quality resolution of civil disputes through the development of forms of arbitration; promotion of goods and services of Russian enterprises in the domestic and foreign markets; dissemination of the principles of civilized business conduct and social responsibility in the business environment; formation of a positive image of Russian producers of goods and services.
